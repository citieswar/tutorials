    Overview¶

    Cities are the core of the game. Every conquest and expansion revolves around controlling cities. The formation of countries, dominions, alliance territories, and even the civilization system—all are built on the foundation of cities.

    Basic Attributes¶

    Each city has three fundamental attributes, and they are derived from real-world data. This ensures that every conquest reflects the true scale and importance of its real-world counterpart.

    • Value: Represents the city’s overall competitiveness, based on real-world rankings of economic and strategic importance.
    • Area: Corresponds to the city’s urban area in the real world.
    • Population: Reflects the actual population data of the real-world city.

    Resource Attributes¶

    Some cities also possess special resources, reflecting their real-world economic or strategic significance.

    • Oil
    • Steel
    • Food
    • Rare Earth
    • Uranium

    Occupying a resource city increases your production rate for that specific resource. Each occupied resource city grants a +10% production bonus to its corresponding resource type.
